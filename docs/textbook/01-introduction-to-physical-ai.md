# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

## Overview
Physical AI combines **digital intelligence** with **physical embodiments** such as robots. In this chapter, we explore the foundations of Physical AI and how it differs from purely software-based AI.

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the concept of embodied intelligence
- Overview of humanoid robotics
- Introduction to simulation and real-world applications

## Key Concepts
- **Embodied AI**
- **Robotics vs Digital AI**
- **Human-robot interaction**
